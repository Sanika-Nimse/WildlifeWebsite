Indian Wildlife Safari Booking System

Welcome to the Online Booking System for the safari in various National Parks and Wildlife sanctuaries in India. This is the one-stop online solution for booking Safari in all the Tiger Reserve forests, Lion Reserve Forest and other Wildlife Reserves in which Safari is organized for the tourists. As all the tourism activities in the National Parks are fully controlled and administered by the respective forest departments. So, the wildlife safari booking service offered here follows the existing guidelines of the particular forest departments. In any of the National Parks in India, visitors can explore the park by the allowed safari modes only within the allowed time periods.

Visitors can select the mode of Safari as per their choice while making a booking. There are four types of wildlife safari are available in the Wildlife Parks in India – Jeep Safari, Canter Safari, Elephant Safari and Boat Safari. The availability of any particular Safari mode varies with the park to park. For example, Ranthambore National Park offers Jeep Safari and Canter Safari for all its zones whereas Corbett National Park allows only Canter Safari in its Dhikala zone and Jeep safari in all other zones. Similarly, the Kaziranga National Park allows Elephant Safari in two of its zones and Jeep Safari in all of its zones whereas only Jeep Safari is available in the Gir National Park in all of its zones. The Sunderbans National Park only allows boat Safari in its safari zones to explore the park so Jeep, Canter or Elephant safari is not available here.

All the National Parks are divided into the ecotourism zones for the better management of the tourism activities. The safaris are allotted a particular zone in any park and they have to stick to their zones while exploring the forest and wildlife. Some of the parks allow their visitors to select the zone of their choice whereas other parks allocate the zones randomly to their safari vehicles. So if any particular park allows the selection of safari zones then you can select the zone while making a booking here otherwise you have to go by their allocation. The entry fees vary with the park to park as well as with the mode of the safari you will choose. The booking fee you will pay here includes the park entry fee, vehicle charge and guide fee. Once the online safari booking is confirmed, it is non-refundable and non-transferable.
